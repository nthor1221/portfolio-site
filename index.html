Nathan Thor

3D Character Artist

My name is Nathan Thor. I am Hmong American and a DMA student pursuing my BFA degree at San Jose State University in 3D modeling. I come from a bloodline of shamans in my animist religion. My experiences with the supernatural has contributed to my passion for the fantasy and horror genre. I like creating works around the fantasy genre as I wish to make life more magical. However, I do wish to explore the horror genre as an artform to tackle darker themes. I am passionate about creating 3D characters in programs such as Blender, Zbrush, and Substance Painter. When I am not making art, I am often playing video games or cooking new recipes.
